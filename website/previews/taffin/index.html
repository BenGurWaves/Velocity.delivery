The Architect of Vision

James de Givenchy founded TAFFIN in 1996 to challenge conventional luxury jewelry. Raised in France, inspired by New York's creative energy, he has spent three decades pioneering sculptural designs in unexpected materials—rubber, ceramic, steel—proving that true luxury lies in artistry and individuality.

His work has been celebrated in a Rizzoli monograph and featured across prestigious publications. But the true measure of his mastery is in the collections of discerning collectors who understand that perfection is imperfection.

"I look for the magical, the right proportions, and I don't find perfection attractive. In personalities, I'm not attracted to flawless either. I look for charm. Jewelry is an object that projects who you are."
