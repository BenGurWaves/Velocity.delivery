The Movement

Kaliber JU26-01. A metamorphosis of horological alchemy.

Based on the Soviet Pobeda calibre 2608. Completely transformed. Hand-applied anglage. Skeletonized train bridge.

Finishing

Every surface finished by hand. 200+ hours of devotion compressed into forty millimeters.
