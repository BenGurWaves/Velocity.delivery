Easy Perpetual

An inventive take on watchmaking's most demanding complication. Sapphire prisms integrated into the caseband refract calendar information — a world-first innovation. Foolproof bi-directional setting through the crown alone.

Case 39mm × 9.5mm Gold
Power Reserve 90 Hours
Price €138,000
Solstitium
2021 — Commission

Solstitium Tourbillon

A double-barrel tourbillon with retrograde minutes and jumping hours. At just 39mm wide and 9mm thin — extraordinarily sleek for such a complex mechanism. Features a spring-driven escape wheel: the watchmaker's own invention.

Complications Tourbillon, Retrograde, Jumping Hours
Power Reserve 75 Hours
Frequency 28,800 BPH
Marie-Elise
2019 — Debut

Marie-Elise

The first fully handmade Dutch wristwatch. Rose gold case with open lugs, silver guilloché dial, and heat-blued steel hands. Features an innovative triangle-shaped pallet fork — lighter and more efficient than conventional designs.

Significance First Dutch Handmade Watch
Jewels 20 (Hand-cut Rubies)
Power Reserve 46 Hours
