Not Jewelry.
Wearable Sculpture.

Since 1977, Joel Arthur Rosenthal has created fewer than 4,000 pieces — each one unique, each one commissioned for a specific client, each one the result of hundreds or thousands of hours of hand fabrication.

There are no collections. No lines. No catalog. Only individual works of art that happen to be worn on the body.

"The Matisse of our time."
— Ellen Barkin
