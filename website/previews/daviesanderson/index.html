Five Generations of Excellence

Founded in 1892 by Edward Davies and James Anderson, our Savile Row atelier has dressed royalty, statesmen, and discerning gentlemen for over 130 years. Our commitment to the highest standards of British tailoring remains unchanged.

Every suit that leaves our workshop represents the culmination of decades of expertise, passed from master to apprentice through generations of craftsmen.
