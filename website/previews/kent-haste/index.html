John Kent, Stephen Lachter and Terry Haste bring together more than a century of combined experience. From our atelier at 7 Sackville Street, we have crafted garments for some of the world's most discerning dressers — including His Royal Highness The Duke of Edinburgh.

Our House Cut features natural shoulders with graceful lines and soft construction — a style refined over decades through careful observation of our clients' preferences.

Years of Mastery

Terry Haste began his apprenticeship at 15 with Anderson & Sheppard. His journey took him through Hawes & Curtis, Tommy Nutter, Hackett, and Huntsman before establishing Kent & Haste. Clients include Jack Nicholson, Elton John, Mick Jagger, and Eric Clapton.

8-12

Weeks to Perfection

Each garment requires three to four fittings. We observe not only your measurements, but your posture and figuration. The process cannot be rushed — we recommend allowing at least 8-12 weeks for true bespoke excellence.

The Process

The Bespoke Experience

Consultation

We discuss your requirements, lifestyle, and preferences to guide fabric and style selection.

Measurements

We record your measures while observing posture and figuration for the perfect pattern.

Fittings

Three to four fittings over 8-12 weeks ensure every detail meets our exacting standards.

Completion

Your garment is finished by our longstanding house tailors — the best makers in the trade.
